FAQ

About our project

How many years has our team been successfully operating in the IT market?

Answer:

We have been operating in the market for over 3 years and have gained considerable experience in web development, system integration and other IT services.

Our achievements are reflected not only in the number of successfully completed projects, but also in long-term partnerships with our clients.

We pride ourselves on delivering innovative solutions and high-quality service to help our clients achieve their technology business goals.

What specific services and products do we provide to our clients?

Answer:

We offer a wide range of services, including web development, mobile applications and database management systems. Our team of highly qualified specialists is capable of implementing projects of any complexity, from creating an attractive and functional web interface to developing complex multi-platform mobile applications.

We also specialise in the development of systems that optimise data management, ensuring efficient access, storage and analysis of information.Our approach is based on the use of advanced technologies and best practices to ensure the highest quality and customer satisfaction.

What are the deadlines for projects or software development?

Answer:

The timing of the task depends on its complexity and scope.

We always put the client first and strive to understand their needs and expectations for the project.

Our team starts with a consultation, where we discuss all the details and requirements in detail to make the process of working together as transparent and efficient as possible.

How do you create customised solutions for your clients?

Answer:

At the initial stage, the manager analyses all aspects of the context and details of the requirements related to the project. This allows us to deeply understand the unique needs and ambitions of each client.

After that, our experts develop individualised strategies that take into account the context and contribute to the successful completion of the task.

This is to ensure that each solution meets the client's needs and expectations to the fullest extent possible, speeding up and delivering quality solutions.

About our team

How many developers are in our head office, what is the level of professional expertise?

Answer:

Currently, our team consists of 12 highly skilled developers specialised in front-end development. They have in-depth knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and have experience with modern frameworks such as React, Angular and Vue.js. They create user interfaces that impress with their aesthetics and functionality, ensuring an effective user experience.

Our backend development team consists of 6 specialists. They are responsible for the development of the server side of applications, databases, and business logic, and have a deep understanding of various programming languages such as Java, Python, C#, PHP, and Node.js. Their knowledge helps ensure reliable and efficient operation of all system components.

We have 5 developers specialised in mobile application development. They have experience with Android and iOS platforms, and use various languages and tools such as Kotlin, Swift, and React Native. Our programmers create applications that provide a convenient and engaging user experience on a variety of mobile devices.

These developers make up our diverse team, which has a wide range of knowledge and experience, enabling us to create high quality and innovative solutions for our clients.

How many years has our team been successfully operating in the IT market?

Answer:

The programmers have more than 5 years of experience in the commercial sphere. It includes deep knowledge of Web development and Mobile app development. This knowledge base allows us to implement a variety of projects with high quality.

We closely follow the trends and advanced technical solutions, so our projects are always modern and meet the highest standards.

What programming languages and technologies do your programmers know?

Answer:

We use a variety of methods according to project requirements and client preferences, including Java, C#, Python, and JavaScript to efficiently implement various functionalities.

Our team has many years of experience with various frameworks and platforms, such as Angular and React, to create modern web interfaces that meet the needs of users.

We have experts in databases such as MySQL, SqlLite and PostgreSql to efficiently manage and store data with high reliability and access speed.

Our technology capabilities are complemented by a deep understanding of business processes, allowing us to develop customised solutions that are optimised for each client's needs.

What methods and tools do we use to control the quality of development?

Answer:

We use testing, debugging, version control systems, as well as internal processes and checks. Automated testing allows us to quickly and reliably verify the functionality, stability and compliance of our solutions.

We also implement various types of testing, such as unit, integration, functional and load testing, to ensure that our products and services are reliable and distinctive.

Other questions

How often does your team update their knowledge and follow the latest technology trends?

Answer:

Every team member is interested in progressing their skills in programming or design. We always follow the latest technological trends and update our knowledge to stay up to date with innovations.

This allows us to offer innovative solutions to our clients and effectively achieve their goals using the most advanced technologies and approaches.

What is your company's approach to data and information security?

Answer:

The security of client data is our priority. We take all appropriate measures to ensure security, including data encryption, access control systems, data backup and recovery, and strict compliance with information security laws and standards.

Do you provide consulting services after the project is completed?

Answer:

Yes, we provide consulting support after the project is completed. Our goal is to make sure that the client is fully satisfied with the results and can effectively use the new solution.

We are ready to provide clarifications, answers to questions and assistance in resolving any issues that may arise after receiving the project.

Is it possible to implement my project with intermediate stages?

Answer:

Absolutely, we believe it is important to ensure transparency and interaction with our clients at all stages of the project.

We offer a structured approach that includes milestones with intermediate deliverables that allow you to not only see, but actively collaborate at every step.
